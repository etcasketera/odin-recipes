Pizza Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Bocconcini Mozzarella
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Basil Leaves
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle
  2. Spread the pizza sauce out on the dough evenly
  3. Put the cherry tomatoes and the basil leaves on the pizza
  4. Put the mozzarella cheeese on the pizza
  5. Pour olive oil on the pizza
  6. Put in the oven
