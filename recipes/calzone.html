Calzone Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Calzone Dough
  • Calzone Sauce
  • Bocconcini Mozzarella
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Basil Leaves
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Roll out the calzone dough into a circle
  2. Spread the calzone sauce out on the dough evenly
  3. Put the cherry tomatoes and the basil leaves on the calzone
  4. Put the mozzarella cheeese on the calzone
  5. Pour olive oil on the calzone
  6. Fold in half and half again
  7. Put in the oven
